Commute

Unexpected delays are just a fact of life whether you are traveling to work or picking up kids from school. Commute is the simple and intuitive way to travel, giving users the power to not only plan ahead, but make smart decisions on the fly about how to leave and get to their destination.

Commute has a number of great features to optimise your journey

Self-Sufficient Alarm Clock

Commute will maximise sleep and minimise fatigue by adjusting the alarm clock to consider traffic events and congestion. Simply, set the time at destination and time required to get ready.

Travel Comparisons

Time comparisons will simplify the decision-making process of choosing a transport medium. Select the optimal transport option to avoid traffic delays and ensure on time arrival..

Self-Learning

No need to enter extensive information. Commute heightens the user experience by learning travel preferences to deliver an interface tailored to suit people's lifestyles.
